    <div class="section-label">Chapter I</div>
    <h2 class="section-title">Product Vision & Scope</h2>

```
<p>TavernOS is a browser-based virtual tabletop (VTT) platform purpose-built for Dungeons & Dragons and compatible TTRPG systems. It unifies everything a gaming group needs into one synchronized environment: an interactive map canvas, full character sheets, real-time video and chat, initiative tracking, and a powerful Dungeon Master control panel — all live-updated for every player at the table simultaneously.</p>

<p>The core philosophy is <strong style="color:var(--gold)">asymmetric by design</strong>: the DM has godlike controls while players get a clean, immersive view tailored to their character. The experience should feel like sitting at a real table, but with powers no physical table could offer.</p>

<h3 class="sub-title">Core Experience Pillars</h3>
<div class="card-grid col2">
  <div class="card">
    <span class="card-icon">🗺</span>
    <div class="card-title">The Living Map</div>
    <p>A real-time shared canvas where the DM imports images, places tokens, reveals fog of war, and players watch the world unfold tile by tile.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <span class="card-icon">📜</span>
    <div class="card-title">Character at Your Side</div>
    <p>Every player's sheet lives in a collapsible side panel — stats, inventory, spells, HP — always in sync with live game state.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <span class="card-icon">🎲</span>
    <div class="card-title">Roll at the Table</div>
    <p>Integrated dice roller with animated results posted to the table log. Modifiers auto-pulled from character stats. Private rolls for DM.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <span class="card-icon">🎬</span>
    <div class="card-title">Face to Face</div>
    <p>Embedded video and voice via LiveKit, with individual mute/deafen controls and a push-to-talk mode that feels native to the session.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <span class="card-icon">⚡</span>
    <div class="card-title">Initiative Command</div>
    <p>A persistent initiative bar shows turn order, highlights the active combatant, and auto-advances — with DM override at any moment.</p>
  </div>
  <div class="card">
    <span class="card-icon">🛡</span>
    <div class="card-title">DM Command Center</div>
    <p>A dedicated DM view with NPC stat blocks, secret notes, hidden map zones, weather overlays, ambient sound control, and encounter builders.</p>
  </div>
</div>
```
